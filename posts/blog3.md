### Design Decisions
After establishing that we wanted to make badminton community our focus and identifying the events system as the platform's standout feature, we then moved onto exploring how users would interact with the platform. The first step we took is our group all sketched a series of low fidelity wireframes of the main pages to test different layouts, interaction priorities and navigation structures as well as see where we were on the same page and instigate different ideas. 

The low fidelity pages were used to explore how different features could be prioritised throughout the platform. Some layouts focused more on forums and community discussions while others prioritised event discovery, maps or profile interactions. This process helped our group identify which interactions felt more valuable and relevant.
