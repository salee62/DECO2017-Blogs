## My interpretation of the Brief:
BlaBla Corp allows us to design a community platform where users with shared interests can connect through specific and tailored experiences. The goal with this project is to design a distinctive feature that adds value beyong already existing platforms. Rather than recreating standard social media features, the focus is on creating something unique that better supports the needs of a specific community. BlaBla Corp has already helped with core systems such as authentication and basic social infrastructure which allows us to focus on the design, interaction and experience of this project instead.

## Functional requirements:
### 1. Content sharing
With social media being very prevalant in today's society, the way to reach other people is through content sharing. Users are able to share posts, ideas, or information which forms the foundation of interaction within the platform. This is an important part of community building, as without content, there would be no communication or engagement between users.


### 2. Content interation
Users must also be able to interact through other user's content (such as reactions) which encourages discussion and connection, allowing users to feel more involved within the community. Interaction transforms the platform from a passive space into an active and engaging environment, which is essential for user participation.

### 3. Usability
Content should be organised in a manner that reflects the community's focus which ensures relevance and ease of navigation. If content is not structured properly, users may find it difficult to locate information, which can reduce engagement. Therefore, usability plays a key role in ensuring that the platform remains accessible, efficient and enjoyable to use.

## Design Decisions
The design brief's most important aspect is creating a unique and standout feature that differentiates from other community platforms.

The feature must:
- Be specific to a certain community.
- Enhance the way users interact or share experiences.
- Provide value different from other generic platforms.

By identifying these features, the design can focus on designing a meaningful and well intergrated interaction rather than building too many features that do not add as much value.

However, focusing on a "standout" feature can limit the scope of other features. Hence, prioritising depth where a feature is refined and meaningful would be beneficial over too many underdeveloped "generic" or "standard" features.

## Reflection
This process shows that building a community platform is not about the amount of features but to create a meaningful and unique experience that establishes clear functional requirements and a clear design focus which will help with future design development.
